How it works

Simple. Secure. On-chain.

Three steps from job post to payment — all enforced by smart contracts, not trust.

01
📋
Client Posts Job
Client posts a job with a USDC budget and locks funds into a Soroban escrow smart contract on the Stellar network.
02
⚡
Freelancer Delivers
Freelancer applies, gets hired, completes the work, and submits for client review — all tracked on-chain.
03
💸
Instant Payment
Client approves the work and USDC is released instantly to the freelancer's Stellar wallet. No delays. No fees.
Features

Built different.

Everything traditional freelance platforms get wrong, SwiftPay gets right.

🔐
Trustless Escrow
Funds are locked in a Soroban smart contract — neither party can touch them until the agreed conditions are met.
🌍
Global & Borderless
Anyone with a Stellar wallet can use SwiftPay. No bank account required, no country restrictions.
⚖️
On-chain Dispute Resolution
If something goes wrong, a transparent dispute contract handles the resolution — no biased platform admins.
💵
Paid in USDC
All payments are in USDC — a stable, dollar-pegged digital currency. No crypto volatility to worry about.
🔓
Fully Open Source
Every line of code is public and auditable. No hidden logic, no black boxes, no platform risk.
🚀
Freighter Wallet Integration
Connect in one click using the Freighter browser extension. No seed phrases exposed, no custody risks.
